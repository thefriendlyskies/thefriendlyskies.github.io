Luxury private jets have become an essential part of celebrity life. Many high-profile figures opt for private jets to accommodate their demanding schedules and global engagements. Unlike commercial flights with restricted routes and public encounters, private jets offer comfort, privacy, and convenience. These jets are often built with high-end facilities tailored to their taste and preferences. 

Ever wonder which celebrities have the most luxurious private jets? Here is a look into the top 10 most expensive private jets used by celebrities.

Now, let us dive into the top 10 most expensive luxury private jets used by celebrities.

## 1. Donald Trump

{{< img src="/11012026/1.png" id="i\_plane" caption="The White House / Wikimedia" alt="President Donald J. Trump and First Lady Melania Trump walk to Air Force One after arriving at Bordeaux-Merignac Airport in Bordeaux en route to Washington, D.C.">}}

The 47th president of the United States, Donald Trump, is best known for his Boeing 757, famously dubbed the Trump Force One. It is estimated to be worth over $100 million, customized with his signature opulence including gold-plated fixtures, leather seating, crystal accents, and a dedicated media and communications suite. The jet served as both a luxury mode of transport and a flying command center during his business and political years.

Besides the Boeing 757, he is also known to fly in a Boeing 747-8 private jet, reportedly valued at around $490 million. This wide-body jumbo jet rivals other presidential aircraft in size, range, and prestige.

## 2. Drake

{{< img src="/11012026/2.png" id="i\_plane" caption="Aero Icarus / Wikimedia" alt="The N767A at LAX.">}} 

Globally famous Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, and producer Drake flew in his own private Boeing 767 called the Air Drake. It was estimated by [Simple Flying](https://simpleflying.com/air-drake-features-list/) that the plane cost a total of $185 million after full luxury upgrade from a cargo plane. Some of these features are a baby-blue-painted exterior, a logo embellishment of his signature “OVO” owl, velvet couches, gold detailing, and a grand entertainment room.  

## 3. Elon Musk

{{< img src="/11012026/3.png" id="i\_plane" caption="Airwolfhound / Wikimedia" alt="Gulfstream G700.">}} 

Elon Musk owns the [Gulfstream G650ER](https://www.businessinsider.com/elon-musk-private-jet-gulfstream-g650er-lifestyle-wealth-photos-2022-4#the-jet-has-four-living-areas-the-main-cabin-comprises-of-10-seats-and-a-sofa-4) as his primary private jet. It is one of the world’s most advanced long-range business jets that costs approximately $70-75 million. This 99 foot long jet has a customizable interior, four living areas, and an interior designed with contrasting pale leather seats and wooden furnishings. It also has 16 oval windows known to be the largest in business aviation. 

Previously, the tech billionaire and CEO of SpaceX and Tesla owned a [Dassault Falcon](https://thefriendlyskies.net/article/history-of-private-jets/) 900, which he then sold in 2006. In addition to the G650ER, Musk’s fleet now includes other Gulfstream models:

- Gulfstream G550 (N272BG): This jet offers a range of 12,500 km (6,750 nm) and a cruising speed of Mach 0.885 for long intercontinental flights.
- Gulfstream G700: The newest addition to Musk’s fleet, valued at around $78 million with a range of 13,890 km (7,500 nm), a top speed of Mach 0.925, five living areas, two bathrooms, and 20 oval windows designed for maximum comfort on extended journeys.

## 4. Kim Kardashian

{{< img src="/11012026/4.png" id="i\_plane" caption="Matti Blume / Wikimedia" alt="Cabin of the G650ER.">}} 

Kim Kardashian is known for her lavish lifestyle and luxury items, including her air travels. According to [TMZ](https://www.tmz.com/photos/image_jpg_20220226_61b32657869d43ee8d264d4e6681f4bb/), the billionaire businesswoman flew in a Gulfstream G650ER private jet that was built from scratch for $95 million in 2019. To bring her dream jet to life, she recruited renowned interior designers Tommy Clements and Waldo Fernandez. They provided extra customizations such as cashmere embellishments, leather seating, enhanced air filtration, and a cream interior, which cost another $45 million, making her jet have a total cost of $150 million.

## 5. Oprah Winfrey

{{< img src="/11012026/5.png" id="i\_plane" caption="Acroterion / Wikimedia" alt="Gulfstream G650 N657FX at Frederick Municipal Airport, Maryland, USA.">}} 

Famous American host Oprah Winfrey flies in a G650ER, manufactured in 2015 by Gulfstream Aerospace Corp. Currently valued at $70 million, Winfrey spent around $10 million dollars to customize it with two luxurious bathrooms, plush convertible leather seating for 16 passengers, as well as living spaces for dining, working, entertaining, and relaxing. It even has a private conference room which can be closed off for meetings.

## 6. Jay-Z & Beyoncé 

{{< img src="/11012026/6.png" id="i\_plane" caption="Acroterion / Wikimedia" alt="2021 Bombardier Global 7500on final approach at Maryland, USA.">}} 

Well-known celebrity couple Jay-Z and Beyoncé currently fly in the [Bombardier Global 7500](https://www.aircharter.com.hk/aircraft-guide/private/bombardier-canada/bombardierglobal7500) registered as N44440. Manufactured by Bombardier in 2023, this aircraft has a significant range of over 14,260 km (7,700 nm), powered by two General Electric Passport turbofan engines to achieve a maximum speed of 0.925 Mach. It is estimated to cost $80 million with modifications. The features and interior of their private jet is unknown, but this particular model is typically split into four interior zones. It also has the Nuage seat, an advanced technology to enhance physical comfort for natural body posture during long flights, and a sophisticated air purification system.

## 7. Kylie Jenner

{{< img src="/11012026/7.png" id="i\_plane" caption="Anna Zvereva / Wikimedia" alt="Private Bombardier Global 7500.">}} 

As another member of the Kardashian family, [Kylie Jenner](https://www.globeair.com/b/the-kardashian-family-and-their-private-jets) is known to have a lifestyle of unparalleled luxury. The socialite spent $72.8 million on a Bombardier pink private jet, the Bombardier Global 7500. Kylie Air’s, as it is called, has amenities including ample crew rest space, a master suite with a full-sized bed and an en-suite bathroom, two other bathrooms, and premium beige leather seats. It also has customisable zones for working, dining, lounging, and sleeping. 

## 8. John Travolta

{{< img src="/11012026/8.png" id="i\_plane" caption="John Murphy / Wikimedia" alt="Travolta's 707 at LAX for the first Qantas A380.">}} 

American actor John Travolta is known to be an aviation enthusiast, with an impressive private jet collection including rare aircraft. Travolta's [Boeing 707](https://simpleflying.com/john-travolta-private-jet-fleet-guide-2025/) was an iconic part of his aviation fleet. Valued at $60 million, it was an ex-Qantas plane that he customized with a retro Qantas livery to honor the airline. He eventually donated it to Australia's Historical Aircraft Restoration Society (HARS) for preservation. According to the latest update in 2025, Travolta owns three registered aircraft models, which are a Dassault Falcon 900B, a [Boeing 737-300](https://thefriendlyskies.net/article/airbus-a220-vs-boeing-737/), as well as the Eclipse EA500 very light aircraft. He is also a licensed pilot with a runway and a parking area for his planes in his estate.

## 9. Taylor Swift

{{< img src="/11012026/9.png" id="i\_plane" caption="N509FZ / Wikimedia" alt="Dassault Falcon 7X">}} 

Taylor Swift is widely known for her successful music career. This globally acclaimed singer-songwriter flies in a [Dassault Falcon 7X private jet](https://www.paramountbusinessjets.com/blog/a-look-inside-taylor-swifts-private-jet) from French manufacturer Dassault Aviation, valued at around $54 million. The plane is known for its high performance, with an ultra-long range of 11,019 km (5,950 nm) and a top speed of Mach 0.90. It also has plush seating, a private stateroom, and a gourmet galley. Previously, she owned other private jets, including the Dassault Falcon 900.

## 10. Celine Dion

{{< img src="/11012026/10.png" id="i\_plane" caption="John Taggart / Wikimedia" alt="Bombardier Global Express XRS at London Heathrow Airport.">}} 

World-renowned pop singer Celine Dion flies in the Bombardier Global Express XRS, a jet with a reach of about 12,038 km (6,500 nautical miles). Its starting price costs over $42 million, but she bought a pre-owned one in 2021. Her plane has convertible leather seats, six guest beds, a small cinema, and a full kitchen. This aircraft lets her celebrity travel long distances without needing a stop.

## Why Do Celebrities Fly in Luxury Private Jets?

{{< img src="/11012026/11.png" id="i\_plane" caption="Monaam Ben Fredj / Wikimedia" alt="Dassault Falcon 900B Registration G-HMEI.">}} 

### 1. Privacy and peace of mind

For public figures, private jets offer a discreet travel experience which minimizes exposure to crowds, paparazzi, and constant attention. This allows celebrities to travel in a calm, low-profile environment.

### 2. Fly at your own pace

Celebrities are known to have hectic schedules which cannot leave room for delays or time constraints. Flying in a private jet gives them the flexibility to take off when they need to without having to depend on commercial flight schedules.

### 3. Comfort and personalization

Private jets can be tailor-made to accommodate their unique preferences and lifestyle. With a lounge area, bedroom, bathroom, and entertainment room, private jets offer a pleasant experience and relaxation for high-profile busy individuals.

### 4. Enhanced security

Beyond luxury, private jets have strict security measures with experienced crews and strict protocols. This helps celebrities avoid any risks that cannot always be controlled in commercial flights.

## Conclusion

Private jets have become a convenient mode of transport for many high-profile figures whose lifestyle demands constant travels, tight itineraries, and enhanced security. Among the figures mentioned, Donald Trump owns the most expensive aircraft, with his Boeing 757 and Boeing 747-8 valued at a combined $590 million, followed by Drake's Boeing 767 at $185 million and Elon Musk's Gulfstream fleet at $153 million. Overall, the private jets featured span a broad price range from $42 million to over half a billion. While the price point and environmental footprint of private air travels are debatable, it is no doubt that private jets offer the kind of privacy and flexibility that celebrities need.
